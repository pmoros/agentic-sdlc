# Archive

This folder contains archived materials that are no longer actively used but retained for historical reference.

## Purpose

Preserve historical information while keeping active folders clean:
- Maintain historical record
- Support auditing and compliance
- Enable reference to past decisions
- Preserve institutional knowledge
- Free up active folders from outdated content

## What to Archive

Move content here when it is:
- **Superseded** - Replaced by newer versions or approaches
- **Completed** - Projects or initiatives that have concluded
- **Deprecated** - Standards or practices no longer in use
- **Historical** - Past decisions or documents of historical value only
- **Inactive** - Not referenced or used for extended period

## Archive Structure

Organize archives to maintain findability:
```
/99-archive/
  ├── YYYY/ (by year)
  │   ├── projects/
  │   ├── decisions/
  │   └── documents/
  ├── deprecated/
  │   ├── processes/
  │   └── standards/
  └── superseded/
      └── architecture/
```

## Archival Process

When archiving content:
1. **Document Reason** - Note why it's being archived
2. **Add Metadata** - Include archive date and reason
3. **Update Links** - Update references in active documents
4. **Maintain Context** - Keep related materials together
5. **Index** - Maintain inventory of archived materials

## Metadata Template

Include in archived documents:
```markdown
---
ARCHIVED: YYYY-MM-DD
REASON: [Superseded | Completed | Deprecated | Historical]
REPLACED_BY: [Link to current version if applicable]
NOTES: [Additional context about archival]
---
```

## Retention Policy

Define retention periods:
- **Legal/Compliance** - Retain as required by law or regulation
- **Financial** - Follow accounting and tax requirements
- **Projects** - Retain for minimum period (e.g., 3-7 years)
- **General** - Review periodically for permanent deletion

## Access

- Archived materials remain accessible for reference
- May have different permissions than active content
- Should be clearly marked to avoid accidental use

## Retrieval

If archived content becomes relevant again:
- Review for currency and accuracy
- Update as needed
- Move back to active folder structure
- Document restoration and updates
